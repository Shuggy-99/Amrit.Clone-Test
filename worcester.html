Worcester Bosch Boilers

Worcester Bosch boilers have been keeping homes warm since 1962 and are committed to delivering the highest standard of heating. There is a reason that Worcester boilers are so incredibly trusted by the general public, because they are a terrific, reliable option when it comes to buying a new boiler.

With a range of gas and oil boilers , hot water cylinders and renewable technologies, you can be sure that Worcester Bosch’s products will integrate seamlessly into your home or business premises, significantly reducing your heating bills thanks to their high efficiency ratings and money saving technology.

It’s no wonder Worcester Bosch is an eleven time winner of the Which? Best Gas and Oil Boilers award, with Which? Claiming: “Worcester Bosch performs so consistently well in our annual reliability surveys that its name is synonymous with dependability.”

Worcester Bosch Boiler Efficiency

We are all taking our effect on the environment more seriously, and boiler efficiency levels can play a huge part in protecting our environment and saving on heating bills. If you want to ensure your central heating output is at an appropriate level, whilst also keeping an eye on your carbon footprint, a Worcester Boiler could be the perfect option for you. A range of the combi, regular and system boilers we can install from Worcester Bosch come with an A-Rated efficiency rating , meaning you won’t ever have to spend extra unnecessary money, and may actually save you money in the long run, compared to an older boiler.

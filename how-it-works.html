Choose your boiler

Our quote engine will learn with every quote the best boiler, and pick only the most suitable boilers for your home.

We will then give you our recommendation alongside budget and premium alternatives. You also have the option to upgrade the kW for home with more hot water demand.

We have a great range of boiler from Viessmann, Worcester Bosch and Alpha with this great range we will always have a boiler to suit any budget.

Select an install date

You can then select a specific day for your install, if you order before 1pm you can even choose next day! (subject to availability)

Depending on the day you select we have discounts available which will be applied directly to your checkout of up to £50.

If there is a specific date that you don't see available on the calendar you can always call the office to see if we can arrange.

Await the cavalry!

After you have completed your order we will ask for you to upload a few images of your current boiler so that we can survey the job and order all the correct materials.

We will call you 1-2 working days before your install to confirm your order and the engineer that will be installing your new boiler.

Your engineer will complete an on-site checklist to ensure he has completed the job to a very high standard and the new boiler has been fully tested and central heating system fully balanced.

A few days after the install we will call just to check how everything went and if we could have done anything better, we are always trying to improve our customer experience.
